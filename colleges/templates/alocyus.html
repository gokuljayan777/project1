St Aloysius College, Elthuruth

Written By: Collegedunia Team

St Aloysius College, located in Elthuruth, Thrissur, is a first-grade college. It is affiliated with the University of Calicut, Kerala, and is also accredited by NAAC. Established in 1968, the college offers sixteen under-graduate and eight postgraduate courses. Some of the popular courses provided include B.Com, BBA, BSc, MSc, BA, MA, etc. It has about 2000 students enrolled in different courses and around 103 faculty members in various departments.

Admissions

Admissions to these courses are provided purely and solely based on merit. The application mode is done entirely online.
