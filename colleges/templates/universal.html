About Universal Engineering College

Written By: Sapna Malik

Universal Engineering College was established in 2011 and is backed by Universal Educational Trust, registered under the Indian Trust Act of 1956 at Ernakulum registrar office. The college aims to provide quality education for those who strive for it. The trust is also committed to supporting financially backward students and promoting the upliftment of socially backward communities.

The institute offers opportunities for personal improvement, intellectual advancement, and professional development, including placement opportunities. Universal Engineering College is approved by AICTE and affiliated with the University of Calicut.

Departments:

  • Department of Civil Engineering
  • Department of Mechanical Engineering
  • Department of Electrical Engineering
  • Department of Electronics and Communication
  • Department of Computer Science and Engineering

Library: The library has more than 5000 volumes of textbooks, over 40 journals/magazines, and access to over 275 e-journals.

Universal Engineering College Admissions

Admissions to all courses are conducted through the Common Admission Process, and students must apply online via the official college website during the application period.
