About Little Flower College, Guruvayoor

Little Flower College, Guruvayoor was established in 1955 by the serving souls of the Franciscan Clarist Congregation and is under the religious jurisdiction of the Roman Catholic Archbishop of Thrissur. It is affiliated with the University of Kerala and approved by the University Grants Commission (UGC). The college offers undergraduate and postgraduate degree programs in arts, science, and commerce fields. The college provides separate accommodation for boys and girls with all modern facilities.

The college maintains a healthy educational environment and provides world-class education through effective and efficient processes. It has a placement cell that offers proper guidance to students and organizes workshops, seminars, and group discussions to enhance students' knowledge and skills.
