About Sahrdaya Institute of Management Studies

Sahrdaya Institute of Management Studies (SIMS) is a premier institute located in Thrissur, Kerala, offering high-quality management education. It is known for its commitment to fostering academic excellence and providing the best practical training opportunities.

The college offers a range of management programs including MBA with different specializations, and aims to develop both academic and practical skills in students to make them industry-ready.

Admissions


Admissions to the MBA program at Sahrdaya Institute of Management Studies are based on merit, with a strong emphasis on academic performance, entrance exams, and personal interviews. The application process is online and requires submitting the necessary documents.
